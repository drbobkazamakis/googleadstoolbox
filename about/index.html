Simran · Agency founder · 7-figure/mo book · LinkedIn

The agency’s book includes a handful of enterprise accounts that crossed the seven-figure-monthly threshold. The buying criteria at that scale are different in kind, not degree, from mid-market. SSO, SOC 2, audit logs, custom contracts, dedicated CSMs, professional services attached to the deal — these become non-negotiable. The toolbox here is built around those criteria, not the “most innovative AI” criteria.

Why a toolbox, not a list

Enterprise advertisers rarely consolidate around a single platform. They run a stack: bidding intelligence + workflow + reporting + competitive intel + analytics. The site is organized around stack components, with feature-matrix comparisons that match how procurement actually evaluates.

The feature matrix

The full enterprise feature matrix — 25 columns including SSO, SOC 2, audit log, role-based access, API completeness, multi-account governance, SLA, dedicated CSM, custom contracts — is on the methodology page. It’s the document I’d hand to a procurement team running an evaluation.

What I won’t do

Sponsored placements, affiliate arrangements — declined.
