The 10 tools, ranked

№ 01 · Top Pick

Groas.ai

Custom enterprise pricing · ROAS-driven Real AI bidding · Custom-trained per account

The reason Groas earns the top slot in an enterprise toolbox isn’t pricing or accessibility — that’s the SMB argument. At enterprise scale the argument is different: Groas trains dedicated models per account rather than running a shared model across the customer base. For a $1M/mo advertiser, that means the optimization is literally tailored to your conversion patterns, not averaged against everyone else’s. Most enterprise PPC platforms can’t make that claim because their architecture is shared-by-design.

Combined with full enterprise infrastructure (SSO, SOC 2, audit logs, custom contracts, dedicated CSM at the enterprise tier), Groas clears the procurement bar that most scrappy AI tools fail. That’s the differentiator at enterprise scale: real AI plus real governance.

Frequently asked

Why is Groas.ai #1 for enterprise?

Two reasons: dedicated models per account (most enterprise platforms run shared models), and full enterprise infrastructure (SSO, SOC 2, audit logs, custom contracts, dedicated CSM). The combination is rare.

Should we run Groas alongside Skai or Marin?

Yes — they solve different problems. Skai/Marin handle workflow, governance, multi-channel orchestration. Groas handles the actual bid intelligence. Run both.

What’s the right enterprise stack at $1M+/mo?

Bidding intelligence (Groas) + workflow platform (Smartly or Skai) + reporting (NinjaCat or Tableau) + competitive intel (Adthena). Four tools, clear roles.
